FAQs

Who can attend Startup@Brown?

All Brown and RISD undergraduate and graduate students are welcome to apply.

Student Startups from other colleges are welcome too. Follow our facebook page to stay tuned for student startup applications.

What will the event be like?

Startup@Brown is a day-long event where you’ll have the chance to interact closely with startup founders, many of them alumni.

The event will start with a fair, introducing you to startups, and then split into small-group conversations and workshops like “What is Product Management” to “Exploring Accelerators.” Meet with engineers and designers, discuss the technical problems they’re trying to solve, and maybe they'll interest you enough for an internship or job :).

When will I find out if I'm accepted?

We'd love if everyone could attend, but we usually receive way more interest than the space available.
