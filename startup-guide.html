FAQ

What are workshops? Can we host one?

A leader, engineer, or designer teaching a prepared 40-min workshop/talk to 25-35 students with 10 minutes for Q&A at the end.

The workshops aren't necessarily interactive and they can range from "what is product management" to "what is social entrepreneurship" to tech talks from engineers. They're usually a slideshow.

Yes! If you're a sponsor and would like to host a workshop, we'd need the following by September 19:

  1. Title of workshop
  2. Name of person(s) teaching the workshop
  3. Title of person(s) in the company (lead designer, CTO, co-founder, engineer, etc.)
  4. Company Represented

No worries if you can't host a talk, some companies don't. We'll still have "Startup Office Hours" where students can come talk with engineers/representatives from the companies for a more casual free-form conversation. Students get to know the companies more in-depth or talk with some they missed at the startup fair.
